🔑 What if I forget my password?
Your password is stored inside your data file — not on any server.

If you forget it: open the app → tap "Forgot password?" on the lock screen → load your saved JSON file → set a new password. Everything restores instantly.

This is why saving your data file regularly is important. It is both your backup and your password recovery key.

Forgot your password?
No problem. Your password is stored inside your data file. Follow these steps to reset it.
1
Find your spend-na-data.json file — it's in your Downloads, Files app, WhatsApp, or Google Drive (wherever you saved it last).
2
Tap "Load my data file" below. Pick your JSON file. The app will recognise it and ask you to set a new password.
3
Your data will be fully restored with a new password of your choice.
— or —
⚠️ If you don't have your data file
Without your data file, the password cannot be recovered. You can clear all data and start fresh.
